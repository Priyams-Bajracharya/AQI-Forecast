Our mission

At AirSense, we're on a mission to provide the most accurate and reliable air quality forecasts. We believe that everyone has the right to breathe clean air, and our goal is to empower people with the information they need to make healthier choices. Our team of data scientists, environmental engineers, and software developers is dedicated to delivering real-time, hyperlocal air quality insights that help you live your best life.

Why we do it

Air pollution is one of the biggest environmental health risks we face today. It's linked to a wide range of health problems, from asthma and heart disease to cancer and cognitive decline. Unfortunately, many people are unaware of the air quality challenges in their area, which makes it difficult to protect themselves and their loved ones. We're passionate about solving this problem by providing easy-to-understand, actionable air quality forecasts to help people make informed decisions about their daily activities.

The science behind our forecasts

We use a combination of cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, weather models, and air quality sensor data to generate our forecasts. Our proprietary technology analyzes historical air quality patterns, current meteorological conditions, and other relevant factors to predict future air quality levels with high accuracy. We continuously validate and improve our models based on the latest scientific research and feedback from our users, ensuring that our forecasts reflect the most up-to-date understanding of air pollution dynamics.

Meet the team

Our multidisciplinary team brings together expertise in data science, environmental engineering, and software development to deliver the best air quality forecasts. We're passionate about using technology to promote public health and environmental sustainability.
